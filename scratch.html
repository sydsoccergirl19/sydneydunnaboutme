My Journey So Far

I was born in New Jersey, but my family has moved frequently throughout my life due to my dad’s work. I lived in North Carolina as a toddler before moving to Washington State, where I stayed until the middle of third grade. From there, we moved to South Carolina until I was in sixth grade, and then returned to Washington. During COVID, my dad’s job became fully remote, which led us to move to Utah.

I graduated from American Fork High School and began attending Brigham Young University before leaving to serve a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in Fresno, California. I returned home in June 2025 and have since resumed my studies at BYU. I am currently pursuing a degree in Supply Chain and am working toward being accepted into the Marriott School of Business for Fall 2026.

My Hobbies and Interests

I have always loved staying active and trying new things. I have played soccer since I was about six years old, and it continues to be one of my favorite sports. My parents also enrolled me in piano lessons when I was young, so I have been playing the piano since childhood.

I enjoy learning new skills and pushing myself outside of my comfort zone. Some of my other hobbies include:

  1. Outdoor hobbies

    • Horseback riding
    • Going to the gym
    • Pickleball
    • Yard work with my family
    • I especially love anything sports-related and spending time outdoors.
  2. Creative hobbies
    • Painting
    • Baking and cooking
    • Playing board games

My Goals and Aspirations

My number one goal in life is to be married in the temple and have a family one day. I feel incredibly blessed by the Lord and am deeply grateful for the many blessings He has given me and my family. I have a strong testimony of His plan of salvation, and I hope to one day be a loving mother who teaches her children about God and His plan.

I also have important academic and personal goals. I am working toward graduating with my bachelor’s degree from the Marriott School of Business. I am very driven and strive to push myself every day. I hope to use the skills and talents I have been given to make a positive impact and give back through my work and education.

In addition, I have several physical and adventure-based goals. I want to run a full marathon, complete a 200-mile bike ride, participate in a Hyrox competition, and go skydiving. More than anything, I hope to build a life full of adventure and meaningful experiences with my family. If you know me, you know I love to have fun and create lasting memories with the people I care about most.
